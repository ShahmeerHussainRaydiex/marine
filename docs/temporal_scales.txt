This txt contains the possible temporal scales that can be used for the marine plan model.
New scales may be added, and these values could change.

hourly_low -- 5th percentile for hourly wind speeds
hourly_high -- 95th percentile for hourly wind speeds

daily_low -- 5th percentile for daily wind speeds
daily_high -- 95th percentile for daily wind speeds

weekly_low -- 5th percentile for weekly wind speeds
weekly_high -- 95th percentile for weekly wind speeds

yearly -- yearly average
